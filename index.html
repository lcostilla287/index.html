The Mysterious Creature Eggdog

There is a majestic creature known to only few as Eggdog. The very existence of this creature balances everything in the universe.

Description

Even though it has hardly ever been seen, some have been able to observe some of its qualities.

Here are some of the observations that some brave individuals have noted.

  • Is about 3 feet tall
  • Is not hostile
  • Highly adaptive
  • Found throughout the world

Unfortunately, only records of this creature have been found. Those who have made these records mysteriously disappeared. Or so we thought...
